Privacy Policy — MedKit

Last updated: 2025-12-21

MedKit is a medicine and kit tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

1. Data We Collect

MedKit does not collect personal data to our servers. The app is designed to work offline.

2. Data Stored on Your Device

The information you enter in the app (for example: medicine name, expiry date, quantity, price, notes, categories, kits) is stored locally on your device.

3. Device Permissions

MedKit does not require access to your contacts, location, or microphone for its core functionality.

If barcode scanning is added in the future, the app may request camera permission only for scanning purposes.

4. Third-Party Sharing

MedKit does not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

5. Data Deletion

You can delete your data by removing medicines/kits inside the app or by uninstalling the app from your device.

6. Children’s Privacy

MedKit is not intended for children under 13.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date above will reflect any changes.
